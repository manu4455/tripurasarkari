TPSC Assistant Controller of Examinations Recruitment 2025

About the Post

Tripura Public Service Commission (TPSC), Agartala has issued a recruitment notification for filling up 01 (one) UR post of Assistant Controller of Examinations, Group-B (Gazetted) on deputation basis. Applications are invited from eligible officers holding analogous posts under State Government Departments / Organizations.

Important Dates

  • Notification Date: 06 December 2025
  • Last Date to Receive Application: 26 December 2025 (5:00 PM)
  • Mode of Application: Offline (By Post / In Person)
Post Name Pay Scale Category
Assistant Controller of Examinations (Group-B, Gazetted) Rs. 47,600 ‚Äì Cell-1 of Level-13 (GP Rs. 4,800) UR (01)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Holding analogous post under State Government Department / Organization.
  • Essential: Graduation from a recognized University.
  • Desirable:
    • Administrative experience
    • Experience in handling examinations & tests
    • Computer working knowledge
    • Fair knowledge in English & Bengali

Selection Process

  • Shortlisting based on API Score (out of 100)
  • Interview: 50 Marks
  • Minimum qualifying marks in interview: 25
  • Final merit list prepared only on interview marks

Period of Appointment

Initial deputation period will be 2 years, extendable if deemed fit and necessary.

How to Apply

  • Apply in plain paper through proper channel.
  • Advance copy with API score sheet and documents is allowed.
  • Applications must reach TPSC, A.K. Road, Agartala.
  • Incomplete or late applications will be rejected.
